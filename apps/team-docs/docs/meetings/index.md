# Meeting Notes

This section contains meeting notes, including standups, sprint planning, retrospectives, and design reviews.

## Creating a New Meeting Note

Click **Edit Content** in the top navigation or go directly to [/admin/#/collections/meeting-notes](/admin/#/collections/meeting-notes) to create a new meeting note.

## Meeting Types

- **Standup** - Daily sync meetings
- **Sprint Planning** - Sprint kickoff and planning sessions
- **Retrospective** - Sprint retrospectives and team reflections
- **Design Review** - Design critiques and feedback sessions
- **Technical Discussion** - Architecture and technical decisions
