# Onboarding Guides

This section contains onboarding guides for new team members.

## Creating a New Onboarding Guide

Click **Edit Content** in the top navigation or go directly to [/admin/#/collections/onboarding](/admin/#/collections/onboarding) to create a new guide.

## Available Guides

Guides are organized by role:
- **Developer** - Engineering setup and workflows
- **Designer** - Design tools and processes
- **Product Manager** - Product processes and stakeholders
- **QA Engineer** - Testing frameworks and procedures
- **All Team Members** - General team information
